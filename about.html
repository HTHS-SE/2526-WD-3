    Our History

  • Founded in 1889

    11 March 1889

    Twilight Airlines was founded in 1889 with the mission to provide unparalleled luxury and comfort in air travel.

  • Airplane invented in 1903

    17 December 1903

    The Wright Brothers Invented the plane, while working for Twilight Airlines. Now that the plane was invented, we were able to start planning flights.

  • First Commertial Flight

    29 Feburary 1921

    Twilight Airlines first flight was on this day and it was a smashing success.

  • Our First Plane Crash

    April 1 1952

    Twilight Airlines experienced their first tragic crash in 1952.
    The pilot used the incorrect number of sig figs in their calculations, and confused the IMA of the engine for AMA.
    Luckily, no lives were lost.

  • Twilight Airlines Cloudhopper Aviation Merger

    17 March 1978

    Twilight Airlines expanded its regional presence by acquiring Cloudhopper
    Aviation, a company known for its scenic but slightly unpredictable mountain routes.
    Integration was smooth, once everyone agreed on a single definition of "cruising altitude".

  • Twilight Airlines Duty-Free

    17 March 1989

    Twilight Airlines introduced its own line of duty-free products, including branded neck pillows, cologne,
    and a popular line of socks, which were later featured in a niche fashion magazine!

  • Twilight Airlines CEO Transition and Strategic Rebranding

    3 January 2006

    A new CEO took the helm and introduced the slogan "Twilight Airlines: Because the Ground Is Overrated"
